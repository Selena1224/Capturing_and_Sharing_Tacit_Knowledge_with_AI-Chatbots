# Using AI-Powered Chatbots to Capture and Share Tacit Knowledge in Organizations

## Overview
Research project examining how AI-powered chatbots and Natural Language Processing (NLP) can capture, preserve, and transfer tacit organizational knowledge.

## Objectives
- Explore the role of conversational AI in knowledge management.
- Analyze methods for converting unstructured knowledge into reusable organizational assets.
- Evaluate implementation challenges and best practices.

## Skills Demonstrated
- Artificial Intelligence (AI)
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Knowledge Management
- Literature Review and Research Synthesis
- Technical Writing
- Business Analysis

## Key Takeaways
This research highlights how conversational AI can support organizational learning by making experiential knowledge more accessible, searchable, and reusable.
